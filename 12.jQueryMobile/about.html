Why jQuery Mobile?

jQuery Mobile makes it easy to build mobile-optimized websites with a consistent look and feel across devices. Some benefits of jQuery Mobile include:

  • Simple to implement
  • Works across all devices and platforms
  • Customizable themes

This is a great tool for developers looking to build responsive mobile sites quickly.
